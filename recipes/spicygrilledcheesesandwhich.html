Spicy Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Who doesn't love a culinary classic with a little bit of spice?? This recipe is a quick one that only takes 5 minutes and will leave you thinking about seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 4 slices white break
  • 2 slices American Cheese
  • 1 roma (plum) tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 small onion, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over low heat.
  2. Spread butter or margarine onto one side of two slices of bread.
  3. Place both pieces of buttered side down on the skillet.
  4. Lay a slice of cheese on each one, and top with slices of tomato, onion and jalapeno.
  5. Butter one side of the ramining slices of bread, and place on top buttered side up.
  6. When the bottom of the sandwhiches are toasted, flip and fry until brown on the other side.
